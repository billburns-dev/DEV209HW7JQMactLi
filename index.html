My Energy Balance is your personal daily energy balance tracker.

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)
Amount of energy body uses for daily essential functions while at rest.

Physical Activity Level (PAL)
A way to quantify daily physical activity. Is used to estimate total daily energy expenditure (TDEE).

Total daily energy expenditure (TDEE)
Estimates the number calories the body burns daily. A method of estimating TDEE is ...
